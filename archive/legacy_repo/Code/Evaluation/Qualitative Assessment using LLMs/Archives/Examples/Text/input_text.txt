The context and goals of the model are as follows. The overall objective of the agent-based model is to simulate urban mobility patterns and the use of various modes of transportation by the population of a mid-sized city. Specifically, the goal of the model is to explore the influence of transportation preferences, availability of public and private transport options, and urban infrastructure on the daily movement and interaction of city residents. The model aims to replicate the changes in transportation behaviors and the usage patterns of different mobility options over time.
