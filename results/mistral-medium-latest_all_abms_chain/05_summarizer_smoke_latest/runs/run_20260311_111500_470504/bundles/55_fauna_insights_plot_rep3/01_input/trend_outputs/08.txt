This trend suggests a rapid extinction event for male animals within the simulated environment. For decision-makers, this indicates that under the given parameters and conditions, the male animal population is not sustainable and faces extinction within the simulated timeframe. This insight could be crucial for understanding the dynamics of hunting pressure and environmental factors on male animal populations and for making informed decisions regarding conservation efforts and hunting regulations.
