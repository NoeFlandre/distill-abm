The mean factor applied to the skimmed and semi-skimmed milk choice function due to habit shows a clear increasing trend over the simulation period. However, from the sixth time step onwards, the habit factor begins to rise steadily. The habit factor reaches its maximum value of 1.5879 at the final time step, indicating a substantial influence of habit on the choice of alternative milk types by the end of the simulation period. This insight can inform strategies aimed at reinforcing positive habits towards healthier milk choices, potentially through targeted marketing campaigns or educational initiatives that highlight the benefits of alternative milk types.
