The inflection points identified suggest multiple phases where the rate of decline changes, indicating shifts in the dynamics of milk consumption behavior among the agents. The presence of peaks and valleys, although not highly prominent, suggests minor fluctuations within the overall downward trajectory. The oscillation indices further support the identification of periodic minor fluctuations amidst the general decline.
