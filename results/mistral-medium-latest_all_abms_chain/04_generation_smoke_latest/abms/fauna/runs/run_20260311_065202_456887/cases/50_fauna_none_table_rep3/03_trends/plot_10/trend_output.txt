The peaks and valleys identified in the data further illustrate these minor fluctuations within the broader context of a declining population. Ultimately, the simulation results in the complete extinction of the prey animals by the final tick, indicating that under the given parameters and conditions, the prey population is unable to sustain itself in the presence of the modeled hunting pressure and environmental factors.
