The cumulative number of male prey animals hunted and killed by forager bands in the Megaherbivore Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM) exhibits distinct trends over the simulation period. Towards the end of the simulation, there is a slight decreasing trend observed from 1856 to 2087 ticks.
