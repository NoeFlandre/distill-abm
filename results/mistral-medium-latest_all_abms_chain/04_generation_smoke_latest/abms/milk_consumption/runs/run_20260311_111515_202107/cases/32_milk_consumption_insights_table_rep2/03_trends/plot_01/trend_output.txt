This downward trend continues, albeit with some fluctuations, until the end of the simulation where the average consumption reaches approximately 253.10 units. The data exhibits several inflection points, indicating changes in the rate of decline, and some minor peaks and valleys, suggesting periods of temporary stabilization or slight reversals in the trend. However, the overall trajectory remains consistently downward. The rolling Mann-Kendall test confirms significant decreasing trends in multiple windows, reinforcing the overall decline in whole milk consumption.
