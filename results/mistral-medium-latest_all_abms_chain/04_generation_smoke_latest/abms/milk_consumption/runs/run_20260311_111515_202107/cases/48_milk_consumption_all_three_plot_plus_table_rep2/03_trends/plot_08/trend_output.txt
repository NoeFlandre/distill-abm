Following this rapid increase, the mean habit factor continues to rise steadily, reaching a value of approximately 1.0502 by the end of the simulation. This steady increase implies a consistent reinforcement of whole milk consumption habits over time. The presence of multiple oscillation indices suggests periodic fluctuations in the habit reinforcement process, potentially reflecting cycles of social influence and habit re-evaluation among the agents.
