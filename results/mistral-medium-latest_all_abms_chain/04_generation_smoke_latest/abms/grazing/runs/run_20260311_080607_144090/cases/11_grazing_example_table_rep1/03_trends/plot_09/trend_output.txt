As the simulation progresses, the rate of increase in destocking gradually slows down, reflecting a more stabilized and steady approach to destocking over time. The simulation concludes with the cumulative number of destocked livestock nearing 97, demonstrating that the majority of destocking activities have been carried out by the end of the simulation period.
