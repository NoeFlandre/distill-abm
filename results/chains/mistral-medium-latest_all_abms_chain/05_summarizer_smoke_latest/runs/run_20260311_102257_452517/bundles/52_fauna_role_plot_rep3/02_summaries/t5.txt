the model is an agent-based model designed to test hypotheses related to the extinction of megaherbivores . the landscape is a simplified grassland environment with randomly generated patches of grass, male and female ungulates, and human hunters . the model allows users to conduct experiments to test how environmental conditions, human hunting, and animal life history interact to influence extinction dynamics . the total energy of foragers begins at a value of 0 and exhibits a slight increase in the initial steps, reaching a peak around 500 units . the cumulative number of deaths among the prey animal population exhibits distinct trends over the simulated time steps .
