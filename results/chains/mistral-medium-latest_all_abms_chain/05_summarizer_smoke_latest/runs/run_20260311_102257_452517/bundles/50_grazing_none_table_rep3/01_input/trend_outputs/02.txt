Following this peak, the deviation exhibits a decreasing trend, which is also statistically significant. This downward trend continues until around time step 50, where the deviation stabilizes and no significant trends are observed for the remainder of the simulation. The latter half of the simulation shows a more stable pattern with minor fluctuations, ending with a deviation value of approximately 0.1911.
