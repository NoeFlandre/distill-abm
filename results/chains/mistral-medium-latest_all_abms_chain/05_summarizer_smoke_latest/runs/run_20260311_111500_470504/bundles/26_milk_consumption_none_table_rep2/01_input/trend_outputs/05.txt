Starting from an initial value of 0, the total number of choices rises uniformly, reaching a final value of 32,000. The mean and median values of the total choices are both 16,000, indicating a symmetric distribution around the center. No peaks, valleys, or inflection points were detected, underscoring the simplicity and uniformity of the trend. This trend reflects a gradual and consistent adoption of milk consumption behaviors among the agents over the simulated period.
