The Megafaunal Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM) is an agent-based model designed to test hypotheses related to the extinction of megaherbivores. The model simulates a simplified grassland environment with human hunters and male and female ungulates. The goal is to understand how environmental conditions, human hunting behavior, and animal life history interact to influence extinction events or create equilibrium conditions. The model allows users to manipulate these parameters to test various hypotheses related to extinction events.
