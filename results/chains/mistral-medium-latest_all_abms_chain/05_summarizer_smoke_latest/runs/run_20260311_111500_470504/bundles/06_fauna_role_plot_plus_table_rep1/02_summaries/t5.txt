the model is an agent-based model designed to test hypotheses related to the extinction of megaherbivores . the landscape is a simplified grassland environment with randomly generated patches of grass, male and female ungulates, and human hunters . the model's landscape consists of an abstract square universe that wraps at the edges . ungulate herds are sexually dimorphic, with distinct food values and processing costs for males and females . animals can reproduce, and users control sex ratios and reproduction probability .
