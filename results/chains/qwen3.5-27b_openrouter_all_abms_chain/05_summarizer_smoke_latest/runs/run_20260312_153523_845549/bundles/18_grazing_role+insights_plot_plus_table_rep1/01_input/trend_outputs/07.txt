Oscillations are observed in the range of timesteps 78 to 93, though the trend remains strongly upward. This trend suggests that inequality in healthy livestock holdings among households grows consistently over time, becoming most pronounced in the later stages of the simulation. For decision makers, this indicates that without intervention, disparities in livestock health outcomes will naturally widen, potentially undermining community resilience and equitable development.
