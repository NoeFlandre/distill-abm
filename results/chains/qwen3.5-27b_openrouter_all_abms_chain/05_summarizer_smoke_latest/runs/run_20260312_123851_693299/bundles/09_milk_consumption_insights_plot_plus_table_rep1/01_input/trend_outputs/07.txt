During the middle phase, spanning roughly from step 11 to step 27, the growth trend flattens into a plateau. This middle phase is characterized by statistically insignificant trends, suggesting that factors driving the initial rapid adoption have temporarily stabilized. In the final phase, beginning around step 27, the consumption trend resumes its upward trajectory with high statistical significance. The average quantity increases sharply from this second plateau to reach a maximum value of 1456.59 at the final step.
