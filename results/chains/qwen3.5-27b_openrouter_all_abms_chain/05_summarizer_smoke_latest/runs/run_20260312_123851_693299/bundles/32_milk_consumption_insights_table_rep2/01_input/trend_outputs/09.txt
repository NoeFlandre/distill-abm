Oscillatory behavior becomes apparent in the later stages, particularly between time steps 17 and 30. For decision makers, this indicates that interventions facilitating the initial trial of lower-fat milk options are critical, as the habit mechanism subsequently amplifies the preference, making the shift self-sustaining once the initial threshold is crossed.
