 The Megafaunal Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM) is an interactive, agent-based simulation designed to test hypotheses regarding megaherbivore . It allows users to simulate a prehistoric landscape to observe how environmental factors and human predation interact to influence population survival . The rapid depletion of energy implies that the female population, crucial for herd reproduction, was unable to sustain itself under the defined hunting intensity .
