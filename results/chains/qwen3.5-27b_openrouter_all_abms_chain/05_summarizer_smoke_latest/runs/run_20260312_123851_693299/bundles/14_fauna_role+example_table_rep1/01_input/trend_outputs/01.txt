The total number of active human hunter-gatherer bands remains fixed at 20 from the start to the end of the simulation. The count shows no variation, with a consistent value of 20.0 across all 2312 time steps and a standard deviation of zero. This stability is a direct result of the immortal-foragers parameter, which prevents demographic changes within the human population. As a consequence, the hunting pressure exerted on the megaherbivore herds remains static throughout the model run, isolating the effects of environmental constraints and predation mechanics from fluctuations in human population size.
