The decline is consistent across the simulation runs during this initial 37-step window, indicating a uniform response among the heterogeneous households to the early environmental conditions. After reaching the trough at step 37, the total livestock count reverses direction and enters a phase of recovery. The trend shifts to increasing around time step 46, with the rate of growth accelerating significantly between steps 46 and 60. This recovery phase suggests that the system has adjusted its stocking rates to a level compatible with available pasture biomass, allowing herds to rebuild.
