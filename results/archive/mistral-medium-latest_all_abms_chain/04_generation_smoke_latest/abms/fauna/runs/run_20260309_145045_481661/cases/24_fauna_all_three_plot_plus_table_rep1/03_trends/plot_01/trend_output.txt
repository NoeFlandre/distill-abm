The number of human hunter-gatherer bands in the simulation remains constant at 20 throughout the entire duration of the simulation. This stability indicates that the parameters set for the simulation, such as the initial number of foragers, their movement cost, and the fact that they are set to be immortal, contribute to a steady-state scenario where the number of active hunter-gatherer bands does not fluctuate. For decision makers, this implies that under the given conditions and parameters, the model predicts a stable population of hunter-gatherer bands, which could be useful for understanding scenarios where external factors do not influence the population dynamics of the foragers.
