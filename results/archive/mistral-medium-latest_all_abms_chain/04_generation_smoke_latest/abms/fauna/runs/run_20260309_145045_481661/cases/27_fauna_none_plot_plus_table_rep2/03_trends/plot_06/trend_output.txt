After reaching the peak, the number of births begins to decline. This decreasing trend is also statistically significant and occurs in two phases: from 1624 to 1855 time steps and from 1856 to 2087 time steps. The data also shows some oscillatory behavior with a dominant period of 50 time steps, suggesting periodic fluctuations in the number of births.
