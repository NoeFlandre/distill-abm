As the simulation progresses, the rate of increase in consumption begins to slow down. The peaks and valleys identified in the data further support this observation, showing small variations around high consumption values. 

Towards the end of the simulation, there is a slight resurgence in the increasing trend of consumption, although the rate of increase is not as pronounced as in the initial stages.
