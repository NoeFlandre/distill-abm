The total energy of all animals in the Megaherbivore Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM) exhibits a consistent and pronounced decline over time. Initially, the total energy of all animals is approximately 25,000 units. This high energy level is maintained for a brief period, followed by a sharp decline starting around the 500th step. By the 1,000th step, the total energy drops significantly to around 5,000 units. This downward trend continues, albeit at a slower rate, with the total energy gradually approaching zero. By the 2,000th step, the total energy of all animals is nearly depleted, indicating a substantial reduction in the energy levels of the prey population over the course of the simulation.
