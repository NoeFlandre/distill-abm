agent-based model aims to simulate the shift from whole milk to skimmed and semi-skimmed milk from 1974 to 2005 . the model compares two variants with different mechanisms for how agents consider their choices . agents represent adult consumers who make milk choices based on perceived health and environmental characteristics . the total number of times agents chose skimmed or semi-skimmed milk in the simulation shows a consistent increasing trend from 1974 to 2005 .
