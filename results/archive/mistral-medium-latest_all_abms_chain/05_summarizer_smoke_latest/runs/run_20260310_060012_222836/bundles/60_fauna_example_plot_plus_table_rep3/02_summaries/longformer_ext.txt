The Megafaunal Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM) is an agent-based model designed to test hypotheses related to megaherbivore extinction. It considers various factors such as environmental change, human hunting behavior, herd demography, movement, animal sexual dimorphism, and life history. The model allows users to manipulate parameters to observe how different conditions interact to influence extinction or equilibrium outcomes.
