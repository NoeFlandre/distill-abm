Over the course of the simulation, this number exhibits a consistent and steady decline. By around 500 steps, the number of male animals has decreased to roughly 80. This downward trend continues, and by approximately 1000 steps, the number of male animals further reduces to about 20. Beyond 1000 steps, the decline becomes more gradual, with the number of male animals approaching zero.
