The model's context involves smallholder farmers making decisions about livestock placement on their privately-owned pastures based on observations of pasture states and neighbor behaviors. The primary goals are to evaluate the long-term social-ecological performance of different behavioral types and to study the diffusion of behavioral strategies in a heterogeneous population.
