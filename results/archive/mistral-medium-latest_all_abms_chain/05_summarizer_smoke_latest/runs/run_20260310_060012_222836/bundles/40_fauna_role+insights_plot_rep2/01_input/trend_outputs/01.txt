In the simulation of the Megaherbivore Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM), the number of human hunter-gatherer bands remains constant throughout the entire duration of the simulation. Initially, the number of foragers is set at 20, and this value does not change over the 73000 ticks of the simulation. This stability indicates that the parameters governing the hunter-gatherer population, such as the immortal-foragers setting being true, ensure that the number of active hunting bands does not fluctuate. For decision makers, this implies that under the given conditions and parameters, the model predicts a stable human hunting pressure on the megaherbivore population throughout the simulation period.
