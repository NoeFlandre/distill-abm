The trend indicates a steady reduction in the male animal population throughout the simulation period. For decision-makers, this trend suggests that under the given parameters and conditions, the male animal population is not sustainable and tends towards extinction over time. This insight can be crucial for understanding the dynamics of hunting pressure and environmental factors on male animal populations and for making informed decisions regarding conservation strategies and resource management.
