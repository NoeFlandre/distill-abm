The percentage of vegetated landscape patches in the Megaherbivore Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM) exhibits a relatively stable trend over the simulation period, with minor fluctuations. The standard deviation is relatively low at 0.950, suggesting minimal variability around the mean. The data shows several peaks and valleys, but these fluctuations are minimal, with prominence values close to zero, suggesting minor variations. This is followed by decreasing trends from ticks 1624 to 2087, again with minimal slopes.
