agent-based model is an extension of the original RAGE (Rangeland Grazing Ecology) model . it simulates interactions between pastures, livestock, and household livelihoods in a common property grazing system . the model aims to evaluate long-term social-ecological consequences of different household behavioral types . the mean reserve biomass in the grazing system starts at an initial value of 90,000 units . in the early stages, there is a slight increase followed by a notable decline . this period of decline is statistically significant, indicating a strong downward trend . the dominant period of oscillation is around 49 time steps . key change points are observed at time steps 30 and 100, marking shifts in the trend dynamics . the mean Gini coefficient over the entire period is 0.0302 . trends in early and middle phases, with periods of no significant trend towards the latter part of the simulation . overall trend shows a reduction in the number of non-learning households over time .
