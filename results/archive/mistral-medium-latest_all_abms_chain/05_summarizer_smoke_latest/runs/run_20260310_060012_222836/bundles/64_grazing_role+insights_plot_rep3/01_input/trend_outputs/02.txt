By around 40 steps, the trends stabilize somewhat, with households generally maintaining their risk attitudes within a narrower range. A few households exhibit significant fluctuations, but most remain relatively stable after the initial divergence. Around step 60, there is another noticeable shift where some households adjust their risk attitudes slightly, but the overall trends remain consistent until the end of the simulation.
