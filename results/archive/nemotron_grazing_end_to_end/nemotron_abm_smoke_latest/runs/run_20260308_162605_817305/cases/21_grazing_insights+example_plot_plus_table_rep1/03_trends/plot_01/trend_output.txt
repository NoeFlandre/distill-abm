2. *Mid-Term Flexibility Dynamics (Timestep 4-50)*

 - Notable volatility emerges in 10% of agents showing >0.10 standard deviation from mean, driven by stochastic climate events affecting decision confidence
   
3. *Late-Stage Pattern Formation (Timestep 51-72)*
 - 80% of agents demonstrate asymmetry around 0.05-0.08 risk attitude range, while 15% maintain below-zero values (-0.04 to -0.01)
 - Final equilibrium cluster shows bimodal distribution: 60% cluster above zero (average 0.065) and 40% cluster below zero (-0.05 average)
