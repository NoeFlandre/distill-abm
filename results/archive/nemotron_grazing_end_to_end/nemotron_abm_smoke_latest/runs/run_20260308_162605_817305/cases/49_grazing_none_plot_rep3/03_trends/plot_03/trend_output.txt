The biomass shows a clear S-shaped trend, representing a series of repetitive trials of  biomass in the pasture ecosystem.  y-axis values start at close  90000, slowly reach a minimum of close to 81000 at step 25, and gradually begins to grow and reach close to 125000 and maintains these values. Most lines are very similar to each other, except for some outliers that do not follow similar trends in the data, reaching values close to the initial values. The result on the y-axis is likely a direct result of overgrazing, degradation in pasture quality, and looking at the long-term data, reversion to the mean is clearly seen.  Values on the y-axis are not a fixed number, but the fluctuation observed is impossible to accurately predict without a time series model representing the underlying stochasticity of the data. Accepting the context of the simulation, with a different set of policies regarding herd management the result could be different.
