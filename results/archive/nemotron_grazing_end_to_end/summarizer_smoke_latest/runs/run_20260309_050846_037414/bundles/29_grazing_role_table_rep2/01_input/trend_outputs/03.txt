Climate variability disproportionately impacts low-reserve biomass years, exacerbating resource stress. This trajectory suggests that without interventions targeting herd reduction, social coordination, or climate adaptation strategies, the simulated system trends toward long-term degradation despite embedded learning mechanisms.
