The number of female prey animals begins at approximately 125 in all simulation runs. A rapid and consistent decline occurs immediately, with the population falling below 20 animals by around 500 time steps in the majority of simulations. This trend suggests that under the current parameters, specifically the combination of forager density and hunting efficiency, the pressure on the female demographic is high enough to drive the population to functional extinction or critical vulnerability within the first 2,000 time steps.
