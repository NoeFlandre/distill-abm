Over the course of 2312 simulation ticks, the number of active human hunter-gatherer bands remained perfectly constant at 20. There was zero variance in the population count from the start to the end of the run. This lack of change indicates that the model parameters disabled forager mortality, maintaining a fixed human population size. For interpretation, this isolates the impact of hunting behavior on the megafauna, confirming that pressure changes are not driven by forager numbers.
