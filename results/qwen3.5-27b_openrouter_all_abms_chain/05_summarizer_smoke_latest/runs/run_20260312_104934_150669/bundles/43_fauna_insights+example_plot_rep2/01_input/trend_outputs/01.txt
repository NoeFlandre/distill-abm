The number of active human hunter-gatherer bands in the simulation is consistently maintained at 20 throughout the duration of the runs. This stability suggests that the simulation conditions provided no mortality or fission pressures to alter the initial population, consistent with the model parameters specifying that foragers are immortal and initial forager counts are set at 20.
