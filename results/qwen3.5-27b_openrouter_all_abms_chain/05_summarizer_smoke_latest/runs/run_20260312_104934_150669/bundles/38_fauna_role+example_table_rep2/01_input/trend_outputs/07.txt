Between steps 1,160 and 1,391, the trend becomes statistically insignificant, marking a transition where the marginal death rate drops. A subsequent decreasing trend is observed between steps 1,856 and 2,087 in the aggregate mean, aligning with the peak and tail-off of the mortality curve.
