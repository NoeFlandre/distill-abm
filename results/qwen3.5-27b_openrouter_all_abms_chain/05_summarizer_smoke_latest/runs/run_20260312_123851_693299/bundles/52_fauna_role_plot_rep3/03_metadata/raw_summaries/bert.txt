ecological system containing human foragers and sexually dimorphic megaherbivore herds. it incorporates principles of optimal foraging theory, where hunters make decisions based on energy costs and food value. goals : the primary goal is to determine conditions under which human hunting pressure, combined with environmental constraints, leads to the extinction of megafauna. the number of human hunter -
