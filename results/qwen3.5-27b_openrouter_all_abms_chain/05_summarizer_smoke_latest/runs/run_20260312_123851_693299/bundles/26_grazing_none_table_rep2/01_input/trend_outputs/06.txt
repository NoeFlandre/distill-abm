By timestep 30, a change point occurs, marking a transition to a lower baseline. From timestep 30 through the remainder of the simulation, the average destocking fluctuates at much lower levels, generally remaining below 1.5. The stabilization at low destocking levels indicates that the learning mechanisms and risk assessments within the model effectively mitigate the pressure on the grazing system after the initial adjustment period.
