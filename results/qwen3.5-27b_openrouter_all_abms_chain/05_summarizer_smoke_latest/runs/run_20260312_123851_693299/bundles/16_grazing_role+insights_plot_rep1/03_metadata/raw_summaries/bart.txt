 The goal is to see how different learning styles affect the health of the community . There are three main ways farmers can learn from their own results (E-RO), learning from each other . The group decided to set up a virtual virtual virtual world of action to create a virtual reality .  Average number of livestock destocked across households begins at 0.0 at the start of the simulation, rapidly increases to a peak of approximately 7.0 within the first few steps, and then declines sharply . By around step 10, the mean destocking levels stabilizes to a range between 1.0 and 3.0 . By step 20 onwards, the values continue to fluctuate slightly, with values spanning between 0.03 and 0.05 across different simulations .  The number of households following the E-RO strategy exhibits a range of behaviors across the simulation steps . For decision-makers, this highlights the importance of understanding how farmers' learning behaviors and environmental conditions interact to shape grazing management outcomes over time .
