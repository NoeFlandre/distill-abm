Context and goals this simulation extends the rage model to study how smallholder farmers manage grazing land when they can learn from their own experience or from neighbors. They decide how many animals to keep based on how the grass is doing and what others are doing. There are three main ways farmers behave : baseline (e - ro), learning from their own results (e - lbd), and learning from neighbors (e - ro - sl1). Satisficing - threshold : range from 0.
