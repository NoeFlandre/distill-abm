The number of active human hunter-gatherer bands remains constant at 20 across the entire simulation period of 2312 time steps. There is no observable fluctuation in the forager count from start to finish. This static trend aligns with the model configuration where forager mortality is disabled, ensuring a fixed baseline of human activity. For decision makers, this indicates that the anthropogenic pressure on the megafauna is held invariant, allowing for a clear isolation of environmental and prey population dynamics independent of predator numbers.
