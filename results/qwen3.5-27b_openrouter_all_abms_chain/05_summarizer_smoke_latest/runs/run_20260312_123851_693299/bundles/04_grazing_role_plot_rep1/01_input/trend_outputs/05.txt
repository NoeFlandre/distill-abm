The average total number of livestock, including both healthy livestock and those that have been destocked, starts at an initial value of 90. A sharp decline occurs within the first 20 time steps, reaching a low of approximately 40 to 41 by time step 40. After this minimum, a divergence in trends is observed among the different simulation runs. Some trajectories show a steady recovery, climbing back up to levels between 50 and 62 by time step 100, while others remain relatively flat near the minimum value of 40 to 43 for the remainder of the simulation period.
