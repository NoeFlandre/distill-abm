The number of human hunter-gatherer bands in the simulation remains constant at 20 throughout the entire duration of the simulation, spanning from step 0 to 2300. This stability is consistent with the model parameter setting 'imortal-foragers' to true, which prevents forager bands from dying or being removed. For decision-makers, this indicates that the human pressure on the megafauna remains static over the modeled timeframe. Consequently, any changes in prey populations observed in the broader model results would be driven by prey demography, environmental conditions, or the efficiency of the hunting strategy, rather than fluctuations in the human population size.
