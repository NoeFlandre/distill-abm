From step 1000 onwards, the percentage of green patches stabilizes near 33%, with minor fluctuations occurring at steps 1100 and 1800. These later variations remain marginal, indicating that the system reaches a quasi-equilibrium where vegetation regrowth keeps pace with consumption. The stabilization of vegetation levels implies a balance between prey population pressures and environmental regeneration capacity.
