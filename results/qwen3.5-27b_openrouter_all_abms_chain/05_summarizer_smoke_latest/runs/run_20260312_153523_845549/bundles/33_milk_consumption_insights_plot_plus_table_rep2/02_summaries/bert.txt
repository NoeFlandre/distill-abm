Context and goals : the overall objective of the agent - based model is to reproduce adoption behaviours of milk consumption by the british public (1974 - 2005), by replicating individual preferences and decision factors. Decision - making follows a basic structure of : agent perception of choice characteristics, the triggering, or not, of disposition to consider alternatives, a set of scored choice functions made up of the perceived characteristics and modulated by habit and social influence, and finally, choice evaluation where agents consider the impact of their choices and may adjust their future decisions. Each agent makes a choice of milk selection based on a function for each alternative, made up of the perceived health and environmental characterises of each choice.
