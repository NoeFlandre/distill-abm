The number of active human hunter-gatherer bands remains constant at 20 throughout the entire simulation period of 2,312 time steps. Beginning at the initial value of 20, the population exhibits no variation over time, maintaining exactly 20 agents with zero variance across all 40 simulation runs. This stability reflects the model configuration in which hunters are initialized at a fixed density of 20, do not reproduce, and are immortal, thereby preventing mortality events from depleting their numbers while also preventing population growth. Consequently, hunting pressure remains constant throughout the simulation duration.
