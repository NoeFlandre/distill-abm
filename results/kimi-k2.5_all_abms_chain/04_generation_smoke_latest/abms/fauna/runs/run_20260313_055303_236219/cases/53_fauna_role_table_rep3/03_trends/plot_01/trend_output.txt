The number of active human hunter-gatherer bands remained constant at 20 throughout the entire simulation duration of 2312 ticks. This static population trajectory reflects the model configuration where foragers are initialized at 20 agents and are subject to the "immortal-foragers" setting, which prevents mortality from energy depletion. With no mechanisms for forager recruitment (birth) or mortality operational in this parameterization, the initial population count persists unchanged across all time steps.
