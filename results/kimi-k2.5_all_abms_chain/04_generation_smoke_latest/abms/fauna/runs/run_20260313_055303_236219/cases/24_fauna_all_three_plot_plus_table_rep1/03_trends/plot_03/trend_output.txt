**Implications for Decision Makers:**

The trajectory reveals that male megaherbivore populations face critical vulnerability within the first two years of hunting pressure, with approximately 60% of eventual total mortality occurring by step 625. Conservation interventions must be implemented before this inflection point, as delayed action leaves insufficient breeding males to maintain population viability.
