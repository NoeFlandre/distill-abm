Inflection points detected at step 316 indicate a transition from this rapid collapse to a moderated decline rate.

The intermediate phase extends from approximately step 350 through step 1,000, characterized by high inter-run variance and stepwise decrement patterns. Between steps 1,000 and 1,500, the population enters a terminal depletion phase. This oscillation superimposes periodic variation on the underlying decline, creating distinctive sawtooth patterns in energy trajectories during the middle extinction phase.
