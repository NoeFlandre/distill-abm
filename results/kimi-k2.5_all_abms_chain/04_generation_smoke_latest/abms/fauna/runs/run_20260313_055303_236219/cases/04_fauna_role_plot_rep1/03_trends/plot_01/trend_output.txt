**Number of Foragers**

The number of active human hunter-gatherers remains constant at 20 throughout the entire simulation period. From initialization (step 0) through approximately step 2,200, the metric exhibits no temporal variation and maintains a fixed value of 20. This stability reflects the model configuration parameter *imortal-foragers* set to True, which precludes forager mortality from energy depletion, combined with the absence of mechanisms for forager recruitment, reproduction, or population turnover in this experimental design. The metric remains locked at the initialization value specified by *init-foragers* without deviation across all simulation runs represented in the data.
