Following the change point at tick 375, the rate of decline attenuates considerably through tick 875, transitioning from slopes of approximately -26.7 units per tick in the initial phase to gentler rates near -0.56 units per tick between ticks 928 and 1,159. The trajectory exhibits notable inflections at ticks 472 and 506, indicating shifts in the curvature of decline as the population structure changes.
