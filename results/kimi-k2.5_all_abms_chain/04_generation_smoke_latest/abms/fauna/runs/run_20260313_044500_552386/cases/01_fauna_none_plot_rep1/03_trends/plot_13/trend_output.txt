After approximately step 1000, the trajectories progressively converge toward minimal energy levels, with most runs falling below 2,000 units by step 1200. From step 1500 onward, total energy stabilizes at or near zero across virtually all simulation runs, persisting in this depleted state through step 2000 and beyond.
