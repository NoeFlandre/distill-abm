Following this initial rapid accumulation, the rate of increase diminishes considerably between steps 500 and 1000, with curves exhibiting a pronounced stepwise pattern punctuated by periods of stagnation. The variance across simulation runs becomes most apparent during this middle phase, with some trajectories plateauing as early as step 600 at values near 108, while others continue accumulating kills until step 1500 or beyond.
