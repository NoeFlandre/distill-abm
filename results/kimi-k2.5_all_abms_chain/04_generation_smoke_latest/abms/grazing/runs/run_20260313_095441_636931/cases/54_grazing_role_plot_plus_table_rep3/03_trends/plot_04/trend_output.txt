From step 27 onward, the biomass demonstrated a consistent increasing trend that continued through the remainder of the 100-step simulation. The rate of increase was particularly steep between approximately steps 33 and 55, after which the growth rate gradually decelerated while remaining positive. Inflection points in the trajectory occurred around steps 9 and 49, marking transitions between phases of accelerating and decelerating change.

By the conclusion of the simulation at step 100, the mean green biomass had risen to approximately 60,535 units, substantially exceeding both the initial starting value and the intermediate minimum.
