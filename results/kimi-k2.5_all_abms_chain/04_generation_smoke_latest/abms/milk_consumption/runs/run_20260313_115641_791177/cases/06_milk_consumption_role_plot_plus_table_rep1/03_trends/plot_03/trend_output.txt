The trajectory of whole milk selection reveals a positively sloped but decelerating accumulation rate over time. The absence of detected peaks or valleys confirms the irreversible nature of the cumulative counting process, while oscillation indices detected near the series terminus (steps 27-30) reflect minor fluctuations in the rate of accumulation rather than directional reversals.
