Growth remains subdued, with cumulative choices staying below approximately 2,500. **Phase 2 (Steps 7–12): Acceleration Phase**

**Phase 3 (Steps 12–32): Linear Growth Phase**

The consistent linearity in the mature phase, rather than asymptotic saturation, suggests that the model maintains a steady-state adoption rate where habitual whole milk consumption persists among a resistant subpopulation that does not fully convert within the 32-step simulation horizon.
