The number of active human hunter-gatherer bands remains constant at 20 throughout the simulation period from step 0 through approximately step 2200. This fixed value reflects the initial parameter configuration of 20 starting foragers (init-foragers = 20) combined with the immortal forager setting (imortal-foragers = True), which prevents hunter death from energy depletion or any other cause. Since hunters do not reproduce and cannot die under these parameters, the hunting pressure remains stable and unchanging over time.
