Agents represent adult consumers positioned randomly within an information environment. Each starting with milk choices reflecting the 1974 market dominance of whole milk versus skimmed varieties. All 1,000 agents exist within a unidirectional social network based on the Watts-Strogatz topology. The rate of accumulation exhibits a decelerating pattern across three distinct phases demarcated by inflection points at time steps 1, 7 and 31. The accumulation begins rising from zero as agents initiate consumption based on high initial incumbent habit strength parameterized at 10.0, reflecting the 1974 market dominance of whole milk. The cumulative number of milk consumption decisions recorded across the agent population exhibits a perfectly linear increase from zero choices at the 1974 starting point to 32,000 total choices by 2005. This consistent accumulation occurs at a fixed rate of exactly 1,000 choices per year throughout the 32-year simulation period. The average weekly consumption of whole milk begins at approximately 2,655 milliliters per person at the outset of the 1974–2005 period, reflecting the historical market dominance of entire milk. The mean habit factor applied to whole milk consumption begins at a baseline value of 1.0000 and increases steadily across the 33-year simulation timeframe, reaching 1.0502 by the final time step. The trajectory exhibits minimal volatility, with values remaining tightly clustered around a mean of 1.0359 and showing a standard deviation of merely 0.0110 across all simulation runs. The measure of choice function heterogeneity begins at 0.0000 in 1974. By 2005, this divergence reaches 0.7508, demonstrating that by the end of the 32-year period, the difference between the highest and lowest choice function scores among agents had grown to approximately 75 percent. This trajectory reveals a monotonic increase in preference diversity throughout the simulation. The deviation increases rapidly from zero to reach values near 13.4. This represents a sharp emergence of cognitive dissonance as environmental awareness spreads through the population. The deviation maintains a sustained plateau, fluctuating slightly within a narrow range.
