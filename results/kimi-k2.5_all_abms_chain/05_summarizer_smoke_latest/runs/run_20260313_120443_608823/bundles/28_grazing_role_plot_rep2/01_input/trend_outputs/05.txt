Between steps 20 and 40, the system stabilizes at a minimum level, with values ranging narrowly between approximately 40 and 45 animals per household. Following step 40, the simulations exhibit marked divergence across replicate runs, indicating variable long-term outcomes depending on stochastic rainfall sequences and learning strategy effectiveness.
