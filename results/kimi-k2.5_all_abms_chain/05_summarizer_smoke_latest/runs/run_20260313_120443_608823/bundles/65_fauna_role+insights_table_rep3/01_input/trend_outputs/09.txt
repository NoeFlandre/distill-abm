**Transient Recoveries**

A second, more pronounced recovery occurred between ticks 1,856 and 2,087 (Kendall's tau = 0.453, p < 0.001), though neither resurgence prevented ultimate extinction at tick 2,311.

**Implications for Decision Makers**
