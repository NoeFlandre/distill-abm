Between steps five and thirty-two, the growth rate diminishes substantially while the metric continues a gradual upward trajectory. This decelerating pattern suggests that while agents continue to diverge in their decision-making functions over the simulated 1974-2005 period, the rate of differentiation decreases as habit formation strengthens and cognitive dissonance mechanisms reach equilibrium states.
