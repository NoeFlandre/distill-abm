This initial jump reflects the activation of the habit formation mechanism from the calibrated starting condition where incumbent-initial-habit equals 10. This sustained growth represents an average increase of roughly 0.001 per step. The deviation registers at zero, indicating complete alignment between agents' health perceptions and their consumption behaviors when whole milk dominates the market.
