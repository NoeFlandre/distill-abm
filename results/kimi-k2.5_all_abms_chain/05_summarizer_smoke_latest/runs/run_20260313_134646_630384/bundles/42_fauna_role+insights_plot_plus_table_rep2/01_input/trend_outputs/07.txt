Oscillation indices clustering between ticks 2170 and 2265 indicate persistent seasonal fluctuations in mortality rates even during the terminal phase of population decline.\n\nImplications for Decision Makers:\nThe model outputs indicate that megaherbivore populations under the specified hunting pressure (20 foragers) and environmental conditions face deterministic collapse within the first 500-850 time steps (approximately 1.4 to 2.3 years given the 365-tick annual calibration), with cumulative mortality eventually encompassing nearly the entire initial cohort.
