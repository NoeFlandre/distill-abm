These agents exist within a social network structure where they continuously make milk selections based on their perceptions of product attributes, existing habits, and social pressures from network connections. Each agent processes information about health and environmental characteristics of milk options, weighs these against their habitual tendencies, and responds to social influence from other consumers in their network.
