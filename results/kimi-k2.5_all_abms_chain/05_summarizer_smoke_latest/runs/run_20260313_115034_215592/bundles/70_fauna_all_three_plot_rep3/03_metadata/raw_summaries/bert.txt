animal's food value, the difficulty of processing it, and how much energy the hunter currently has stored. the model can simulate seasonal environments where grass availability fluctuates throughout the year, and users can control the proportion of the landscape that initially contains vegetation as well as how quickly that vegetation returns after being eaten. maximum - lifespan ranges from 0 to
