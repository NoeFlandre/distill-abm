Following this early peak, the system enters an extended decline phase spanning approximately years 8 through 92. This decline represents a net reduction of approximately 26% from the initial population, translating to the loss of roughly 5-6 non-learning households over the century. The trajectory is marked by change points around years 20 and 45, which demarcate shifts in the rate of decline.
