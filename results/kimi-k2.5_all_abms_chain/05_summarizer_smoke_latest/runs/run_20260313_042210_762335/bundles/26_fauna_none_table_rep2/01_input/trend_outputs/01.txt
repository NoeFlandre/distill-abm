The number of active human hunter-gatherer bands maintains a constant value of 20 across all 2,312 time steps of the simulation. Beginning at the initial tick and persisting through the terminal time step, the hunter population exhibits no variation, remaining fixed at the starting density. This absolute stability corresponds to the model's parameterization of immortal foragers that do not reproduce and are immune to energy-depletion mortality, thereby preserving the initial population of 20 bands throughout the experimental duration without fluctuation.
