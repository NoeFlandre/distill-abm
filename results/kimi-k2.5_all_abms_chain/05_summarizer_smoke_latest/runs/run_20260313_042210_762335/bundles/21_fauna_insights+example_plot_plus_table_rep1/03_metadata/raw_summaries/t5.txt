the megafaunal Hunting Pressure Model (MHPM) is an interactive, agent-based model designed to conduct experiments testing hypotheses regarding megaherbivore extinction . it integrates environmental change, human hunting behavior, herd demography, animal sexual dimorphism, and animal life history . the model aims to examine how interactions between environmental conditions and human hunting pressure influence extinction dynamics . the number of active human hunter-gatherer bands remains constant at 20 throughout the simulation duration from step 0 through step 2312 . this value exhibits population dynamics . between steps 500 and 1200, the accumulation saturates, with most simulation runs asymptoting at values between 110 and 125 individuals . the median cumulative kill count reaches
