The model specifically incorporates sexual dimorphism. Animals-gain-from-food ranges from 0 to 100, set at 1.5, representing energy obtained from grazing. Threshold for starvation mortality, animals-starvation-threshold, ranges between 0 and 100. Movement costs energy, with animals-movement-cost ranging from 1 to 10.
